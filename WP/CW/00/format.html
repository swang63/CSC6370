Formatting text to appear on a Web page is therefore different from formatting text to appear

Activity 6: Graphics

Faig Ahmed is a contemporary visual artist that uses textiles as his visual media. Ahmed's artworks engage the viewers through it's unexpected marriage of traditional crafts, steeped in history, with hyper-contemporary, digitally distorted images often in the form of pixilation, three-dimensional shapes and melting paint that alters the pattern on the rugs. He employs computers to sketch his works and chooses intricate traditional methods of carpet-weaving techniques to transfer his designs on carpets.
